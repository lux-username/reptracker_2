## What was done

- Reviewed **#35** (address autocomplete) against the code: Geocodio (`lib/geocodio.ts`,
  `app/AddressLookup.tsx`) is a single-lookup geocoder with no typeahead endpoint.
- Walked the three realistic approaches (debounced Geocodio / local ZIP dataset /
  third-party typeahead) and their privacy + cost tradeoffs.
- Verified Geocodio pricing from the docs: 2,500 credits/day free, $1/1,000 overage,
  and `fields=cd` is a field append = 2 credits per call. Computed that debounced
  autocomplete cuts the free runway ~3× (~1,250 → ~400 lookups/day).
- Owner decided to **nix #35** — the headroom cut isn't worth it for a tool aimed at
  moderate real use.
- Filed **#41** for anti-bot/abuse protection, grounded in the `app/actions.ts` attack
  surface; likely MVP is Upstash per-IP rate limiting + a global daily-credit circuit
  breaker.
- Rewrote STATUS.md; appended the decision to `decisions.md`.

## Decisions (one line each — full rationale in decisions.md)

- Not building address autocomplete (#35): debounced Geocodio was the only
  privacy-safe option and it ~3×'s the API burn, shrinking the free runway from
  ~1,250 to ~400 lookups/day — not worth it for a moderate-use civic tool.
